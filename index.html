About Me

I am Sachini Kumarathunga, a proficient web developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. I specialize in creating responsive, efficient, and visually engaging web applications.My technical skill set spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for front-end development, supported by back-end expertise in Node.js, PHP, and Python. I am also adept in popular frameworks and libraries, including React, Angular, and WordPress, enabling me to develop scalable and dynamic user interfaces.

In addition to my development skills, I bring experience in data visualization through Power BI, allowing me to incorporate analytics and data-driven insights to enhance user engagement and support decision-making. A key achievement in my career was the design and implementation of a centralized control room for Brandix’s production monitoring—an innovative solution that was the first of its kind in Sri Lanka’s apparel industry.

My technical and academic pursuits have also led me to a groundbreaking project in Sri Lanka’s medical IT sector, where I developed a deep learning model for breast cancer classification, including subtype identification and localization. This project earned an 'A' grade and secured a position in an international competition, showcasing the application of AI in healthcare. To deliver this solution to clinicians, I built a user-friendly React-based web interface supported by a Flask backend, providing enhanced diagnostic support.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends, ensuring that each project I work on leverages the most effective and innovative technologies available.
